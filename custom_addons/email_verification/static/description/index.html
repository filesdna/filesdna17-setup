Restrict check-out for unverified accounts in Odoo!!

The involvement of the internet has increased in the lives of human beings. This also led to a surge in cyber fraud and hacking activities. There are many people who try to place orders on the website illegally.

To overcome this problem, Odoo Website Email Verification Module enables you to restrict these unverified accounts from creating an order on your Odoo website.

The module allows only verified emails to place orders from your website. Customers have to go through an email verification process. For that, the module sends token-based emails to the customers on signup. Email verification also becomes necessary for fraud prevention on your website.

Characteristics

  • It sends token-based emails to the customers on signup.

  • It helps to restrict check-out for unverified accounts.

  • It also helps to send customizable token-based emails for verification to the customers while signup.

    Send Token-Based Emails For Verification

  • Set the validity of the tokens in the configuration on your own.
  • Send token-based emails for verification to the customers while signup.
  • Resend the email if the verifying link is expired or the user wants a new email.

    Email Verification To Activate The Account

  • Accounts will not be activated until the email is verified.
  • On clicking the link sent to the mail the user will be redirected to a verification page.
  • Automatic redirection to login after clicking on the valid email link.
