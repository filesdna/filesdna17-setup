Law Management Features

Advanced Dashboard

Dashboard displays static case details, status graphs for matters, lawyers, and judges.

Case

Easily manage case details and reasons associated with case matters for efficient organization and workflow management.

Case Trial Management

Manage case trials, including scheduling hearings, assigning lawyers, and tracking tasks for subsequent proceedings.

Lawyer Management

Simple process to designate partners as lawyers: in partner view, enable 'Is lawyer' option and set up wage arrangements.

Case Evidence

Easily track every evidence with detailed information, including favoritism and all relevant documents pertaining to the matter.

Invoicing Management

Handle case trial invoicing, including generation, tracking, and management of invoices for legal proceedings.

Act / Articles Management

Law management: Act/Article details, categories, explanations of legal provisions, implications, all in comprehensive format.

Law Practice Area

Specify lawyer's expertise, aid in case assignment, provide specialized legal services tailored to client needs.

CRM & Leads Integration

Efficiently manage law firm workflows and operations with integrated CRM for seamless client requests and leads.

Client Management

Efficiently organize, communicate, and track client-related activities and information with law firm's client management feature.

Project & Task Management

Easily create tasks, plan activities as needed, enabling effective management of projects and tasks within the system.

Document Management

Easily manage required documents related to case matters within the system for streamlined document management and accessibility.

Parties: Witness, Victims, Favors

Manage parties involved in legal proceedings including witnesses, victims, and individuals providing favors within the system.

Case Report

Easily generate PDF reports for any case or matter, including comprehensive details, for documentation or sharing purposes.

Case Category

Efficiently organize case matters into categories and subcategories for better management and navigation within the system.

Email Notifications

Automatically send email notifications upon closure of a law case matter, keeping clients informed of case status.
